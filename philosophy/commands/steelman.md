The following position is being advocated. Construct the STRONGEST possible counter-argument. This must not be a straw man — it must be the argument that the most thoughtful, well-informed, good-faith opponent would make. Make it genuinely challenging.

Position: {{input_text}}

Output format:
**The strongest counter-argument:** [3-5 sentences presenting the most compelling opposing case]

**The evidence that supports it:** [What facts, precedents, or logic support the counter-argument]

**Why a reasonable person could hold this view:** [What legitimate values or priorities drive the opposing position]
